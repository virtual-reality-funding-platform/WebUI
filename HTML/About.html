About Us

Who We Are

SIXR is a group committed to providing virtual reality content to the global community. Our Cinematic VR team is based in Seattle, Washington, and attract talent from the Pacific Northwest to spark creative growth for the VR industry. We provide a platform for VR entrepreneurs to seek funding for their projects, and for investors to find the right project to put their money in. Together, we strive to create a new frontier by expanding the world of virtual reality and making it accessible to anyone and everyone.

What We Do

At SIXR, we seek to accomplish two goals; to attract talented developers looking to build new and exciting VR projects, and to provide a way for those developers to have their projects funded and make their dreams come true. This is accomplished by seeking 'angel investors', or people who are looking for the next up and coming project to invest in. By combining these people into one platform, We allow VR developers to easily get their projects funded, and for investors to track the progress of their investment in real time by seeing it come alive right on our webpage.

Where Do You Come In?

Are you a new VR developer with exciting ideas but struggle to fund your project? Head on over to our fellowship page to learn about our team, or click the 'Submit' button at the top of the page to get your project funded today. Or maybe you're a savvy investor looking to cash in on the rise of VR technology in today's market? Head on over to the 'Showcase' page to take a look at current projects that we host, then click the 'Fund' button at the top of the page to start your partnership with one of our developers today.
